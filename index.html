How to add to your phone's home screen:
1. Tap COPY LINK above.
2. Open Safari (iPhone) or Chrome (Android) and paste the link.
3. Tap the Share button → Add to Home Screen → Add.
4. Done! Open it from your home screen — your setup loads automatically, just log in.
⚠ Keep this link private — anyone with it can access your Client Pulse data.
